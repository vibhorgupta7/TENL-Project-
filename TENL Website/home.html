TENL Program Mission

Teach humbly. Teach joyfully. Teach and learn something new every day.

TENL Program Vision

Empowered people who empower others through a passion for languages, desire for positive connections, and a love of teaching and learning in our multilingual world.

ABOUT US

The Teaching English as a New Language Program (also known as TESOL) equips individuals with the knowledge, skills, and pre-professional education needed to effectively work with culturally and linguistically diverse populations and for careers in teaching the English language in the USA or abroad. With mentorship from our faculty, students will also be prepared for career paths related to applied linguistics, international development, and indigenous language education. Our program is accredited, recognized by the Indiana Department of Education, and meets the TESOL International Association’s Standards for Initial TESOL Pre-K-12 Teacher Preparation Programs.

WHO IS THIS TENL PROGRAM FOR?

Undergraduate and graduate students who are interested in teaching the English language

In-service teachers who want to obtain ELL certification/ add-on ELL licensure

Individuals who want a career in TESOL or work abroad in the education and business sectors

Individuals who have an interest in studying the syntax of the English language and language acquisition

Individuals who want to make a positive difference in the lives of their fellow classmates and their broader community

Individuals who care about multilingual learners and their families

WHAT MAKES US UNIQUE

Experiential Learning

Students gain teaching experience by leading their own English language classes online through our English Language Partners (ELP) program.

Exchange Opportunities

Expanding partnerships and exchange opportunities with schools and organizations in Asia and South America.

Tools for Students

We are a small program which provides an opportunity for all the students enrolled to closely work directly with the faculty.
