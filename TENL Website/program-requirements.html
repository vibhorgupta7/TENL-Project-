Program Requirements

The TENL program can be added to your major or taken as a stand-alone credential. TENL courses can be completed in 3-4 semesters (1.5-2 years).

Home Program Requirements

Degree-seeking students


  1. Undergraduate (Bachelor’s)

For those who want to teach abroad


  1. Your major + TENL certificate

For those who want to teach in U.S. schools


  1. Bachelor's of Science in Education + TENL (i.e. ELL) licensure

Degree-seeking students


  1. Graduate/Post-baccalaureate (Master’s)

*Please be aware that there is a separate admissions process for Purdue Graduate Programs. Consult with the Graduate Studies office before adding the TENL certificate to your studies.

For those who want to teach abroad


  1. Your Master's program + TENL certificate

Non-degree seeking students


For those who want to teach abroad


  1. TENL certificate

For those who want to teach in U.S. schools


  1. Teachers who already hold a teaching license complete the TENL program to qualify for the add-on license in the content area of “Teachers of English Learners”.
  2. Individuals who would like to teach in the U.S. but do not yet have their teaching license should first consider the Transition to Teaching program before adding the TENL certificate.
