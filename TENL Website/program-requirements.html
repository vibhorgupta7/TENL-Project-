Program Requirements

The TENL program can be added to your major or taken as a stand-alone credential. TENL courses can be completed in 3-4 semesters (1.5-2 years).

For those who want to teach in U.S. schools

Teachers who already hold a teaching license complete the TENL program to qualify for the add-on license in the content area of “Teachers of English Learners”.

Individuals who would like to teach in the U.S. but do not yet have their teaching license should first consider the Transition to Teaching program before adding the TENL certificate.
